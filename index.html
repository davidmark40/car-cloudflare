Tips for Renting a Car in Dubai

  • Driver’s License: Make sure you have a valid driver’s license. If you’re a tourist, an International Driving Permit (IDP) may be required, depending on your country of residence. Double-check before booking.
  • Age Restrictions: The minimum age to rent a car in Dubai is usually 21, though some rental companies may have a higher minimum age. Make sure you meet the age requirements before you book.
  • Insurance: While renting a car, you’ll be offered various insurance options. It’s important to know what’s covered and whether you need extra insurance for peace of mind.
  • Traffic Laws: Familiarize yourself with the traffic laws in Dubai. Dubai is known for its strict traffic regulations, and violations can result in hefty fines. Drive safely, and always follow the rules.
  • Fuel: Most car rentals in Dubai come with a full tank of fuel. When you return the car, make sure to refill it to the same level to avoid additional charges.
